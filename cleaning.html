FAQ

Common questions about our cleaning

What's included in a standard cleaning?
Our standard cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms (sinks, toilets, tubs, mirrors), kitchen cleaning (countertops, appliances, sink), emptying trash, and making beds. We follow a detailed checklist to ensure nothing is missed.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all! Many of our clients provide us with access (key, lockbox, or door code) and go about their day. All of our team members are fully background-checked and insured for your peace of mind.
What cleaning products do you use?
We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, we're happy to accommodate — just let us know when booking.
How do you handle satisfaction issues?
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with any aspect of our cleaning, let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to address the issue at no extra charge. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
How much does a cleaning cost?
Pricing depends on the size of your space, the type of cleaning, and any add-on services. We provide free, no-obligation quotes — just contact us with your details and we'll get back to you within the hour with a transparent price.
